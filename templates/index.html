OUR PRODUCTS

PP Woven Bags & Fabric

PP Woven Fabric is material made of thermoplastic resins. These resins are produced by the process of polymerization. PP Woven Bags are easy to clean, store and destroy. Being a weather-resistant material, you can use the PP Woven bags throughou the year. They are long-lasting and reusable making it environment-friendly packaging material.

HDPE Woven Bags & Fabric

HDPE is one of the most durable and versatile thermoplastics in today's market. It is made under controlled conditions by applying intense heat to petroleum. It is even recyclable. It is used in variety of materials like bottles, automotive parts, furniture and different construction products.
